# Company Policies

## Annual Leave Policy
Full-time employees are entitled to 25 days of paid annual leave per year, excluding public holidays. Leave must be requested at least 2 weeks in advance and approved by your line manager.

## Remote Work Policy
Employees may work remotely up to 3 days per week. Full-time remote work requires approval from HR and the department head.

## Expense Reimbursement
Employees can claim expenses related to business travel, meals, and accommodation. Claims must be submitted within 30 days using the companyâ€™s expense portal, with receipts attached.

## Sick Leave Policy
Employees are entitled to up to 10 paid sick days per year. A medical certificate is required if the sick leave exceeds 2 consecutive days.

## Travel Policy
Domestic travel must be booked through the company portal. International travel requires approval from both the manager and the travel coordinator.

## IT Support
For hardware or software issues, employees must raise a ticket via the IT Helpdesk portal. Support is available Monday to Friday, 9am to 6pm.

## Refund Policy for Cancelled Flights
If a company-approved flight is cancelled, employees are entitled to a full refund. Proof of cancellation must be provided. Refunds will be processed within 14 business days.
